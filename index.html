who am i?

that's a pretty good question! does anybody really know who they are? do you?

i'm jackie and i'm a philosophy and political science student in the College of Arts and Sciences at Cornell University (go big red!), which means i get paid in credit hours to argue with people :)

i write things (sometimes people even read them). this website is basically my corner of the internet where i figure things out in public... essays, thoughts on politics and philosophy, maybe some rants about things that bother me. it's kinda my public journal, except i can't blame anyone but myself if you're reading this. i also do some creative writing from time to time, when i'm feeling particularly ambitious


anche, parlo italiano! al momento, sto studiando per ottenere la Certificazione di Italiano come Lingua Straniera (CILS). contattami se vuoi parlare!

what am i working on?

right now i'm juggling a lot (aren't we all?). most of my time is split between coursework at Cornell and writing about political theory and civil rights. i'm also a member of the criminal justice committee for the Cornell University chapter of the American Civil Liberties Union because civil liberties are pretty cool and somebody's gotta fight the good fight! i also serve as the Special Events Committee Chair for the Cornell Anime Club, come check us out!


starting in the spring of 2026, i'll be editing for Logos, Cornell's undergraduate philosophy journal, which is a fancy way of saying i get to read a lot of essays about things like "what is being?" :)
